# Project-Analisis-Data-Populasi-Dunia
Analysis of worldwide population data from the year 1970 until 2022 to achieve the complete the objectives below:
- Analysis of population trend in the aforementioned time frame
- Identification of the correlation between population density, area of a region, and growth rate
- Analysis of population distribution based on continents
- Identify the countries with the slowest and fastest growrth rate
- Prediction of future population trend based on available growth rate

The analysis is then presented in the form of an interactive dashboard.
